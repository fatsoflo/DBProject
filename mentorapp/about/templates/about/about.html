Mentor Meet is the first project created by our team NTFs. We are comprised of Natalia Harrow, Terri Thampan, and Florence Fong.

Aside from being an acronym of our names, NTFs is short for Neurotrophic factors. Neurotrophic factors are biomolecules that help promote the growth and survival in neurons (brain cells), and also with the formation of long-term memories. Likewise, we wish to help promote the growth of students and help nurture them as they take their first steps into the working world- as well as help them create memorable moments with the mentors they find along the way.
